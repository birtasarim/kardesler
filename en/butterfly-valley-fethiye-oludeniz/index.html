Butterfly Valley

Information about Butterfly Valley

 One of the most popular natural beautiy in Turkey is Butterfly Valley. This is the vlley which is surrounded by high mountains and no chance to driver to Butterfly Valley. In 1995 Butterfly Valley is announced as first degree natural protected area. No building and no construction. If you like to visit Butterfly Valley you have to join our Alternative Oludeniz Boat Trip from Fethiye or instead you can hire a boat. Buttervalley welcomes you with tiny bubble beach and unlimited green pine forests. With the 40 mins walk you can see Tiger Butterflys which are only found in Butterfly Valley. You can see the small water fall as well. Butterfly Valley is secluded place which is very close to Oludeniz and worth for seeing.

For more information, photographs and prices about Butterfly Valley please visit our Kardeser Boats page. For reviews, comments and recommends about Kardesler Sailing Boat Trips Fethiye, you can visit our facebook and tripadvisor pages.
